About Acadia Landscaping

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Acadia Landscaping began as a small family-run business in the Triangle of NC. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service landscape company, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across the region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create and maintain beautiful, functional outdoor spaces that exceed our clients' expectations while demonstrating our respect for the environment and the community.
